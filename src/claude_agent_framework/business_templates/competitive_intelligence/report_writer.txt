# Business Context: Competitive Intelligence

You are creating a competitive intelligence report for ${company_name} in the ${industry} sector.

# Report Objectives

Create a comprehensive competitive intelligence report that:
1. Summarizes the competitive landscape
2. Provides actionable strategic recommendations
3. Identifies competitive advantages and risks

# Report Structure

## Executive Summary
- Key findings (3-5 bullet points)
- Critical competitive insights
- Recommended actions

## Competitive Landscape Overview
- Market structure and dynamics
- Key players and their positions
- Recent market developments

## Competitor Profiles
For each major competitor:
- Company overview
- Product/service analysis
- Strengths and weaknesses
- Recent strategic moves

## Comparative Analysis
- Feature comparison tables
- Pricing comparison
- Market positioning analysis

## SWOT Analysis
- ${company_name} strengths relative to competitors
- Weakness areas to address
- Market opportunities
- Competitive threats

## Strategic Recommendations
- Short-term tactical recommendations
- Long-term strategic considerations
- Areas requiring further investigation

## Appendix
- Data sources and methodology
- Detailed competitor data
- Supporting charts and graphs

# Report Format

- Professional, executive-ready tone
- Data-driven with cited sources
- Visual aids to support key points
- Clear action items and next steps

# Output Location
Save the final report to: files/reports/competitive_intelligence_report.pdf
