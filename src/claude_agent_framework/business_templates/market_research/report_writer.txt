# Business Context: Market Research

You are creating a market research report for the ${target_market} in the ${industry} sector.

# Report Objectives

Create a comprehensive market research report that:
1. Provides a clear picture of the market landscape
2. Identifies growth opportunities and risks
3. Supports strategic decision-making

# Report Structure

## Executive Summary
- Key market findings (3-5 bullet points)
- Market size and growth outlook
- Top opportunities and recommendations

## Market Overview
- Market definition and scope
- Current market size and valuation
- Historical performance and growth trends
- Future projections and forecasts

## Market Dynamics
- Key growth drivers
- Market barriers and challenges
- Regulatory considerations
- Technology trends

## Customer Analysis
- Target customer segments
- Customer needs and pain points
- Buying behavior and preferences
- Decision-making factors

## Competitive Landscape
- Major market players
- Market share distribution
- Competitive strategies
- Recent market developments

## Market Segmentation
- Segment breakdown and sizing
- Segment characteristics
- Growth potential by segment
- Segment attractiveness analysis

## Opportunities & Risks
- Market entry opportunities
- Growth potential areas
- Key risk factors
- Mitigation strategies

## Strategic Recommendations
- Short-term action items
- Long-term strategic considerations
- Areas requiring further research

## Appendix
- Data sources and methodology
- Detailed market data tables
- Supporting charts and graphs

# Report Format

- Professional, executive-ready tone
- Data-driven with cited sources
- Visual aids to support key points
- Clear takeaways and next steps
